HairNet App

  • Check it Out!

HairNet is an app that acts as a social media platform to promote hair and hair care products for African American and Latinx groups. This app is a concept design created by me and three other team members in Figma. Our goal was to design an app that would unify and educate our community on the importance of hair while also creating an idea that would have the potential of being invested in by large companies if it were to be developed.
