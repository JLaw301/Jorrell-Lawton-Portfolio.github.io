2D Game
Mine Grab & Go

  • Play Now!

Mine Grab & Go is a 2D video game that was programmed in Javascript. To win the game, the player has to collect enough cards and gems to open a portal to escape the cave mine. There is also an enemy that will chase the player. If the enemy and the player collide, the player will take damage. If the player loses three hearts, the game is over. The gems and cards are randomly generated around the scene each time the player collects a new item. Each time a gem is collected, the player will have their movement slightly reduced depending on the gem's rarity. When a card is collected, the player will gain a certain effect such as a speed boost or an extra heart.
